I have designed and coded a Branch-and-Price (BaP) algorithm to solve the Bin-Packing Problem in Julia after having studied this technique during my courses. The challenge of BaP algorithms is that there exist only few frameworks healing to develop it and that it is often needed to create a custom algorithm depending on the problem.
