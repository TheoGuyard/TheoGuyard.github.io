I worked a bit on Active-set methods during my [Turbo screening](https://theoguyard.github.io/portfolio/2020-09-01-turbo-screening%20copy/) project and I found this method very interesting. I decided to try to code it and it seems to be very efficient to tackle the LASSO ! The code can be found in my [Github repository](https://github.com/TheoGuyard/ActSet). 

For the theoretical part, take a look at *Nocedal, J., & Wright, S. (2006). Numerical optimization. Springer Science & Business Media*, it is a very good book. 

I may write a little pdf to explain a bit the method but my code is enough documented to well understand how everything works.
