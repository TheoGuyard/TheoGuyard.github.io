I am currently finishing my fifth year at [INSA Rennes](https://www.insa-rennes.fr/), in the [Applied Mathematics Department](https://www.insa-rennes.fr/gm.html). I am doing a six-month internship under the supervision of [Clement Elvira](https://c-elvira.github.io) and [Cedric Herzet](https://people.rennes.inria.fr/Cedric.Herzet/Cedric.Herzet/Main.html) at [CentraleSupelec](https://www.centralesupelec.fr). Next year, I'll be in PhD with them.

I followed a specialized research curriculum which enabled me to work with researchers for two years. In 2019, I was able to work on Screening rules for LASSO at INRIA with [Cedric Herzet](https://people.rennes.inria.fr/Cedric.Herzet/Cedric.Herzet/Main.html) and in 2020, I worked with [Jeremy Omer](https://jeremyomer.wixsite.com/recherche) and [Ayse Nur Arslan](https://www.researchgate.net/profile/Ayse-Arslan-7) on building Stochastic models for Kidney Exchange Programs.

Main research topics:
- Optimization
- Machine Learning
- Signal Processing
- Sparse linear models
- Screening rules
- Dictionary learning
