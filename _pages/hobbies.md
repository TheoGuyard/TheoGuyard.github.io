Hobbies
======

**Music :** I started to play the trumpet at 8 years old and I still play it today. I first played classical music, but I quickly moved on to jazz. I also started to play the piano at 15 years old.

**Trek and hiking :** A few years ago, I was not at all interested in hiking. In 2017, I planned to do the [GR20](http://www.le-gr20.fr), a 15 day hike in Corsica's mountains, with my day. From that day on, I can't stop. The following years, I went in Island with two friends to do the [Landmannalaugar](https://www.voyage-islande.fr/le-trek-landmannalaugar-thorsmork-art-162.html) trek. It was amazing. I have also done a part of the [GR34](https://www.mongr.fr/trouver-prochaine-randonnee/itineraire/gr-34-le-plus-maritime-des-sentiers-de-grande-randonnee) in 2019 but also most of the hiking trails of the Southern Alps. My last trek was the [Tour du Mont Blanc](http://www.autourdumontblanc.com/fr/index.cfm/le-tour-du-mont-blanc-tmb.html) in 2020.

**Biking :** In the summer 2020, I decided with two friends to bike along a part of the [Vélodyssée](https://www.lavelodyssee.com), from Bordeaux to Rennes. Unfortunately, we started together but I had to finish the journey alone, but it was a great experience and I'll continue to plan biking trip in the future for sure !

**Eco-building :** Since few years, I have been thinking about constructing my own house one day. I really like container's houses and I often search about how they are made. As for eco-building, I am really interested in making my own garden whenever possible.
