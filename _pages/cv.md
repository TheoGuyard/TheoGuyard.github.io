## Experiences

* `2021` **\| Research internship at CentraleSupelec :**
  As end-of-study internship for my schooling at [INSA Rennes](https://www.insa-rennes.fr), I worked under the supervision of [Clement Elvira](https://c-elvira.github.io) at [CentraleSupelec](https://www.centralesupelec.fr) and also with [Cedric Herzet](https://people.rennes.inria.fr/Cedric.Herzet/Cedric.Herzet/Main.html). The aim of the internship was to develop screening methods, originally designed for convex problems, for non-convex Quadratic Mixed-Integer problems related to the L0-penalized regression. It was also the opportunity to start working on my future PhD subject.

* `2020` **\| Research internship at INRIA Rennes :**

After a year of collaboration with [Cedric Herzet](https://people.rennes.inria.fr/Cedric.Herzet/Cedric.Herzet/Main.html) and [Clement Elvira](https://c-elvira.github.io) from [INRIA Rennes](https://www.inria.fr/fr/centre-inria-rennes-bretagne-atlantique), I made a three months internship with them to conclude our research work on new Screening methods to improve algorithms dealing with the LASSO problem. 

* `2019` **\| Research internship at IRMAR :**
  During three months, I worked with [Jeremy Omer](https://jeremyomer.wixsite.com/recherche) from [IRMAR](https://irmar.univ-rennes1.fr) on an Operation Research problem that I suggested to him. The aim was to find the optimal path for a groomer in a ski station. Although I had no knowledge about Operation Research and Discrete Optimization, I managed to design and code a Branch-and-Price algorithm for this practical problem. It was my first step into the world of research.
